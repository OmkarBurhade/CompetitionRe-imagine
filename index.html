Enjoy!

Enjoying life and enjoying the moment. That's what Maaza's all about, providing great quality and authentic taste experiences for everybody to indulge in. All Maaza products are made from natural, single-strength purees, containing no artificial preservatives, colors, or flavors. Characterized by its high juice content and superior quality in 13 delicious flavors and 9 different forms of packaging.

Tropical juice drink.

This special blend of various fruits with dominant banana overtones has been one of Maaza's most popular flavors for many years.

Mango juice drink.

Maaza Mango, a pioneer among mango drinks, remains its most popular flavor.

Guava juice drink.

Guava, a pear-shaped fruit, is known for its delicate taste, which is like a combination of pear and strawberry

Our story

Our fruits go through various steps before they can be enjoyed as Maaza fruit drinks. In every step of the journey, protecting our superior quality is key .

The Fruti orchards

To make sure we use only the best quality of fruit for our Maaza products, we monitor our fruit orchards with great care.

Producing the best quality tropical fruit drinks

To ensure that Maaza products are of the highest attainable quality, our fruit purees are processed only in the best production locations.

Our goal

Maaza's mission is to make the best quality tropical fruit drinks - available everywhere at affordable prices, so everyone can enjoy them.
